About FLOYDVANAVIE

A focused, appointment‑only showroom dedicated to honest garments with history — repaired where needed, ready to wear. The name nods to Pink Floyd + Nirvana + “vie” for life.

What I look for

American workwear, denim, sportswear, and silver/turquoise spanning the 1930s–1990s. I prioritize construction, wear, and repair potential over hype.
