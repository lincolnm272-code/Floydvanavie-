Who I Am

I’ve always been drawn to the stories behind what we wear — the hands that stitched it, the lives that wore it, and the history woven into every thread. My name is Lincoln, and I started FLOYDVANAVIE to collect, preserve, and share pieces of Americana — from 1930s workwear to 1970s denim, hunting jackets, and beyond.

What FLOYDVANAVIE Is

FLOYDVANAVIE is a curated archive of garments, textiles, and artifacts from the 1930s–1970s. Inspired by heritage workwear, railroad culture, and classic Americana, this collection reflects a love for craftsmanship and storytelling. Appointments are a chance to explore pieces in person, try them on, and connect with where they’ve been.
