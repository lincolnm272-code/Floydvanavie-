Lincoln -- FLOYDVANAVIE

I started FLOYDVANAVIE to keep honest garments in circulation -- pieces with miles, repair, and story. I buy, sell, trade, and repair vintage workwear, denim, and sportswear from the 1930s--1990s.

The name mixes Pink Floyd + Nirvana + vie for life. I grew up around hard-wearing clothing and Americana -- the stuff built to work, then keep working. Appointments are one-on-one so I can pull in your size and interests.
